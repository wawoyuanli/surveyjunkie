Project Manager

Project Managers at Quest Global Research work closely with various online teams in a coordination role, providing project management for studies and ensuring proper execution for all surveys. In this role you will be working closely with other internal departments, as well as client groups. The role requires project coordination and customer relationship skills. Candidates must live in the GTA as the position is located in Oakville, Ontario. A resume must be included with your application.
