marketplace

Tools for Online Market Research and more. Everyone benefits from data-driven knowledge. Our customers come to us for online research tools to empower their insights. Lucid’s survey solutions deliver powerful answers from real people – for market research, advertising campaign measurement and more.

Marketplace Buyers

Marketplace buyers may be consulting firms, market researchers, policy researchers, academics, or anyone else who needs to survey a specific audience. When you use our survey sampling tools, Lucid’s intuitive platform will connect you with respondents who match your desired survey criteria.

Marketplace Supply

If you have an online community that can participate in paid surveys, you could be a Marketplace supplier. Lucid’s community of suppliers are often survey panels, loyalty or rewards programs, online apps, or website publishers that want to engage users with survey opportunities.

Media Measurement

Lucid helps agencies, brands, and media buyers measure the effectiveness of their advertising. Impact Measurement, Lucid’s real-time analytics product, uses a survey-based methodology to measure the impact of digital advertising on brand lift, so you can optimize a campaign while it’s in-flight.
